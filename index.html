Carlos A. Correia’s solo practice explores the private space by investigating individual identity in relation to collective identity within political and social contexts, both local and global. Using repetition as a core technique, his work delves deeply into themes of labor, hierarchy, and social norms. Often functioning as a critical commentary on contemporary societal structures.
